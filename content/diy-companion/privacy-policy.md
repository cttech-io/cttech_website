# Privacy Policy

**DIY Companion** — developed by cttech.io
Last updated: May 2026

## Summary

DIY Companion does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing happens entirely on your device.

---

## Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, share, or sell any data of any kind, including but not limited to:

- Personal information (name, email, location)
- Usage analytics or behavioural data
- Crash reports sent to our servers
- Audio recordings or microphone data
- Camera images or video

---

## Device Permissions

DIY Companion requests the following permissions solely to provide app functionality:

**Microphone** — used in real time to measure ambient sound levels for the Noise Meter tool. Audio is processed locally on your device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

**Camera** — used only to control the rear camera flash for the Torch / Flashlight tool. No photos or video are captured.

**Motion sensors** — the accelerometer and magnetometer are used for the Spirit Level and Wall Scanner tools respectively. Motion data is processed locally and never leaves your device.

---

## Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores only your in-app settings (theme preference, default measurement units, haptic feedback preference) in your device's local storage using standard iOS mechanisms. This data never leaves your device.

---

## Third-Party Services

DIY Companion does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

---

## Children

DIY Companion does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.
