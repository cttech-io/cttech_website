Hi, I'm Charlie.

I work in DevOps, where I spend my time managing large-scale infrastructure. It's interesting work, but I find that I enjoy technology a lot more when I can get my hands dirty with it. 

Whether it’s building [DIY Companion](/diy-companion/)—a small app to help with home projects—or tinkering with servers in my basement, I’ve always preferred building things over just maintaining them.

### What to expect here

This site is pretty simple. It’s just a place for me to write down what I’m working on:

*   **App Development:** Updates on the small tools I build.
*   **Homelab:** Notes on my home server setup, automation, and self-hosted services.
*   **Photography:** Just some photos I take when I'm out and about.
*   **Consumer Tech:** Quick thoughts on the gadgets and software I'm using.

I'm not trying to be an "influencer" or change the world—I just wanted a spot on the web that's actually mine. If you’re interested in similar projects, I’m glad you’re here.

Feel free to browse around.

— Charlie
